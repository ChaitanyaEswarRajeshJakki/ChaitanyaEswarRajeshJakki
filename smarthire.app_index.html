How does it work?

Explore SmartHire Video Interview Platform with no AI, only HI (Human Intelligence)

Step 1

CREATE AN ACCOUNT

Create a set of questions you want the candidates to answer and you are all set.

Step 2

CREATE THE LINK

Create, Share and Post the Link along with your Job Ad on your Job Boards/Broadcast, LinkedIn or any other social media platform.

Step 3

HIRE’EM

Wait for the right candidates to take the video interview. Screen and Hire.

Core App Features

Quick and Easy

Saving time and ease of use has been one of the core design philosophies behind Smarthire. Our simple and intuitive user interface gets you up and running instantly.

Mobile Friendly:

The video interview works across all devices. It doesn’t matter if you do not have a webcam on a PC, you can easily use your Phone. Smarthire works across all devices.

Pricing

Simple product at an insanely low price. Affordable for everyone – Startups to Established ones

Cloud Based Storage

Cloud based storage of video responses makes it accessible anywhere, anytime

Consistent Interview

Same set of interview questions can be given to multiple candidates giving the same opportunity and assessment

Convenient for Candidates

Candidates don’t have to miss the opportunity because of their busy schedule. They can attend interviews at their own convenience.

Convenient for Hiring Managers

We believe hiring is a team effort. Share video interviews, review candidates, compare responses, decide for a two way or in-person interview and take the final decision to HIRE.

No more scheduling

Candidates can respond to your interview questions on their own time so you don’t have to worry about scheduling.

Upcoming Features

Hotlist

Hiring managers can select recorded interviews of the candidates as a list and share it with prospective employers. So that they can watch the recorded interviews and can ask for two way or in-person interview for final selection.

Live Interview

A live interview is a job interview that takes place remotely and uses live video technology as the communication medium

Personalised Branding

You can personalize the branding and give the candidates an experience of your company. Make it unique. Make it yours.
